History of Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur, also known as Spurs, is one of England's most storied football clubs, founded in 1882.

  1. Famous Achievements
    • First British team to win a European trophy (1963).
    • Multiple FA Cup victories.
    • Premier League top-four finishes.
  2. Stadium: Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, London.
  3. Iconic Rivalry: Arsenal FC (North London Derby).

Current Star Players

  • Heung-Min Son
  • James Maddison
  • Richarlison
